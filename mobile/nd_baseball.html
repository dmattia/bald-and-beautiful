IRISH BASEBALL FIGHTS CANCER

On Tuesday, February 25th the Notre Dame Fighting Irish Baseball Team hosted its 13th Annual Meet the Team Dinner. Following the dinner the Irish baseball players shaved their heads to support The Bald & the Beautiful and The St. Baldrick's Foundation.

The team raised over $18,500 for the St. Baldrick's Foundation through the event! You can still donate to support your favorite players and also come to watch. A special thanks goes out to all those who participated in the event!
